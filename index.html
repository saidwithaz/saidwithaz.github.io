Probablistic Generation of Ragtime Music
Research, Programming, Writing
Academic research project meant to convert popular songs to ragtime style.
Reads a database of ragtime melodies, extracts note arrangements, and stores them.
Probabilistically applies this data to a new input song, transforming it into ragtime.
Results were compiled in a research paper and presented at an undergraduate research symposium.
